PART 9.8

ROKI Train (IRL Exercise Habit Tracker)

NEW IN v3 — a daily ritual habit tracker tied to real-world exercise, giving temporary in-game boosts.

#9.8.1 Concept

ROKI Train is a daily ritual surface that gives temporary in-game boosts in exchange for the player doing real-world exercises. The design goal is habit formation and brand affinity, not fitness verification.

#9.8.2 The Two Daily Exercises

  • 50 push-ups → +1 damage to a chosen zone for that day only
  • 50 squats → +5 HP to a chosen zone for that day only

#9.8.3 How Counting Works

  • A 60-second window opens per exercise.
  • The player can tap a button in rhythm with each rep, OR shake the phone (accelerometer detects rhythmic motion).
  • 50 ticks within the window completes the exercise.
  • After completing, the player picks which zone gets the boost.

#9.8.4 No Anti-Cheat by Design

The mechanic does not try to prove that the player actually did the push-ups. The point is not fitness verification — it is the daily ritual. If someone cheats by tapping the button 50 times without doing the pushups, they cheat themselves; the brand still wins because the player engaged with the app daily either way. Ritual over policing.

#9.8.5 Boosts Expire Daily

  • All Train boosts expire at the next 00:00 UTC.
  • No rollover. Strictly daily.
  • No permanent stat impact on the NFT comes from the exercise output itself — only from streak achievements.

#9.8.6 The Streak Counter

  • Completing at least one of the two exercises per day increments the daily Train streak.
  • A missed day resets the streak to 0.
  • The streak is the actual retention hook — not the per-day boost.

#9.8.7 Streak Achievements (NFT-only, permanent badges)

Streak badges are permanent on the NFT but have NO combat impact in Clash. They are badges of discipline, not stat bonuses.

  • 30-day streak: Disciplined
  • 100-day streak: Devoted
  • 365-day streak: The Iron Heart

#9.8.8 Tier Access

  • All tiers can use Train.
  • NFT holders earn permanent streak badges on their Fighter Legacy.
  • Free and Paid tier streaks are tracked but not written to permanent records.

#9.8.9 Halal Alignment

Real physical training is encouraged in Islam (multiple hadith reference the virtue of physical strength). The mechanic rewards self-discipline and daily commitment to the body — a deliberate ecosystem-wide tone consistent with the project's halal-conscious design.
