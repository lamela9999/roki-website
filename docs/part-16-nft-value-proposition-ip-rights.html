PART 16

NFT Value Proposition & IP Rights

#16.1 Core Positioning

NFT holders are not buying PFPs. They are buying equity-like stakes in a growing franchise.

#16.2 The Complete NFT Value Stack

  • Full commercial IP rights to their specific character
  • Access to all 110 techniques including Legendaries
  • NFT Grand Championship qualification
  • Fighter Legacy (permanent inheritable record)
  • Canonical lore in the AI-generated ROKI Verse
  • Permanent achievements: Still Mind, The Unyielding, Iron Gaze, Untouchable, The Mountain, Disciplined / Devoted / The Iron Heart, and future milestones
  • Clan membership with narrative weight
  • Copy Ninja or Mirror Fighter elite status (for the 100 elite NFTs)
  • Priority access to all future airdrops
  • Priority access to all future collaboration benefits
  • All future ecosystem rewards default to NFT priority
  • Launchpad fee benefits
  • ROKI Lands entry when the strategy game launches
  • Historical significance — origin holders of the ecosystem

#16.3 Commercial IP Rights (Bored Ape Model, Adapted)

  • Create and sell merchandise featuring their specific ROKI
  • Use their ROKI in their own creative projects
  • License their ROKI to third parties
  • Build businesses around their specific character
  • Profit from derivative work (100% of revenue)

#16.4 License Scope

License applies to the specific NFT the holder owns. Cannot commercialize other holders' NFTs without permission. Can reference the broader ROKI universe in derivative work. Cannot claim official endorsement unless partnered. License transfers with the NFT on secondary sale.

#16.5 Content Restrictions

No hate speech, illegal content, or content that violates platform terms. No content that damages the ROKI brand. Violations may result in license revocation for the specific NFT.

#16.6 Airdrop & Benefit Policy

Airdrops and benefits flow to CURRENT NFT HOLDERS at the time of distribution, not to original minters. Preserves secondary market value.

#16.7 Long-Term Implication

The ‘NFT holders are center of ecosystem’ framing is a binding implicit contract. All future products must either include NFT holders or justify why they don't.
