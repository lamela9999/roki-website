PART 7

Player Tier Structure

This tier structure is a core part of the ROKI go-to-market strategy.

#7.1 The Three Tiers (Plus Token-Staker Sub-Tier)

Free Tier

  • Account-based (email/password), no wallet required initially
  • 300 HP total (75 per zone)
  • 3 technique slots
  • Common techniques only (no Rares, no Legendaries)
  • No permanent achievements
  • ELO uncapped — can climb as high as skill allows
  • Matchmakes by ELO range against anyone, regardless of tier
  • Can compete in Open Bracket tournaments only
  • Can receive crypto prizes (must connect a wallet at claim time)

Paid Tier

  • Account-based, one-time purchase ($20-50 range — to be finalized)
  • 400 HP total (100 per zone) — matches NFT stats
  • 5 technique slots — matches NFT slot count
  • Commons and Rares only (no Legendaries)
  • No permanent achievements
  • ELO uncapped
  • Free re-rolls of character
  • Can compete in Open Bracket only (unless they also stake tokens)

Paid Tier + Token Stakers

  • Paid tier character + stake a specified amount of $ROKI ($50-100 range being considered)
  • Minimum stake duration: 2 weeks
  • Unlocks access to the Challenger Cup
  • Tokens locked during tournament season; cannot be sold without forfeiting qualification

NFT Tier

  • Owns one or more ROKI NFTs
  • 400 HP total (100 per zone)
  • 5 technique slots (6 for Copy Ninjas)
  • Full access: Commons, Rares, Legendaries
  • All permanent achievements available
  • Full Fighter Legacy tracking
  • Canonical lore in the ROKI Verse
  • One-time Reforging option (see Part 15)
  • Can compete in NFT Grand Championship
  • Full commercial IP rights to their specific ROKI (see Part 16)
  • Priority access to airdrops, partnerships, and all future ecosystem benefits

#7.3 Philosophy of Tiering

The tier system honors three forms of commitment: time and skill (any tier can climb ELO), ecosystem investment (Paid + Token Staker shows financial commitment without NFT prices), and long-term stake (NFT holders are equity participants). No tier is humiliated. Every tier has meaningful recognition and meaningful upgrade paths.

#7.4 Rookie League Onboarding

When the Free tier opens (Phase 2 of launch), new free players start in a temporary Rookie League against other new players. After 20–30 matches, they graduate into the main ELO pool.

#7.5 NFT-vs-NFT Matchmaking Priority

When multiple player tiers share the same ELO range, the system prioritizes NFT-vs-NFT matchups.

#7.6 Visual Distinction in Game

NFT fighters display a visual marker in-game (border, badge, or similar) that differentiates them from paid and free fighters.
