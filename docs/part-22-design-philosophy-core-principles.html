PART 22

Design Philosophy & Core Principles

#22.1 The ROKI Design Philosophy

Every design decision in ROKI traces back to a small set of core principles. These principles are non-negotiable and should guide all future decisions.

#22.2 Halal-Conscious Design

  • No gambling mechanics (no loot boxes, no random paid rewards)
  • No maysir-style competitions where entry fees fund prize pools
  • Prize pools funded by treasury/sponsors, not pooled player entries
  • No riba dynamics — no staking-for-yield-created-from-thin-air
  • No gharar (deceptive uncertainty) in disclosures
  • Transparent treasury operations, disclosed pre-launch
  • Sheikh consultation planned for any gray-area tokenomics decisions

#22.2.1 Halal Philosophy Clarification (v3)

The halal-by-design framework is specifically about what is practiced or endorsed, not about what is depicted. This distinction matters for the character art, the lore, and the broader creative palette.

  • Real haram items are removed from the collection. Cigars, alcohol, gambling mechanics — out.
  • Cultural and fictional depictions of other belief systems and archetypes are acceptable. Priest, monk, wizard, dracula clothing, and similar character-flavor archetypes are fine. Sharia permits depicting other cultures; the believer simply doesn't have to believe in them.
  • The operative distinction is between fictional or cultural representation (acceptable) and practice or endorsement (not acceptable).
  • Sihr — real occult magic practice — would be unacceptable. Cartoonish wizard depictions used for character flavor are fine. The difference is promoting the thing vs. representing that the thing exists in the world.
  • The framework is consistent with how individual cuts have been made so far: cigars were removed from the collection; tarot cards were renamed to neutral ‘Cards’; paper talismans were renamed to ‘Paper Seals.’ None of these were absolute bans on imagery — they were calibrations around what the name or object endorses vs. represents.
  • New characters and items should be evaluated through this lens: is the item practicing haram, or is it flavor for a fictional world? Flavor is fine; practice is not.

#22.3 Skill Over Time

  • Record-based leaderboards, not volume-based
  • Zen rewards the player with the best single run, not the one who plays most
  • Dedication rewards (Dojo) are capped
  • A 14-year-old with unlimited time cannot overtake a skilled adult through raw hours

#22.4 Dedication Capped, Not Infinite

  • Dojo conditioning maxes at +5 HP total, spread across zones
  • Once maxed, no more dedication rewards
  • The Unyielding title achievable by anyone willing to commit moderate time
  • Minigame streak badges (Train, Breath) are badges of honor, NOT combat bonuses
  • No ‘pay-to-win through time’ dynamics

#22.5 No Pay-to-Win

  • NFTs give access, not guaranteed victory
  • A skilled free-tier player can beat a maxed NFT through better strategy and reads
  • Tokens stake for qualification access, not for combat advantages
  • No purchasable stat bonuses. No purchasable technique slots.

#22.6 Dignity for All Characters

  • Losing ROKIs receive dignified lore
  • No lore calls a ROKI ‘bad’ or ‘terrible’
  • Every ROKI gets career snapshots — none are invisible
  • No ‘hall of shame’ or public humiliation surfaces

#22.7 Transparency Over Secrecy

  • Treasury operations disclosed pre-launch
  • Tokenomics published in detail
  • Development progress visible (GitHub, dev log, shipped updates)
  • No hidden insider mechanics

#22.8 Ship Before Polish

  • Rough Clash with 50 players teaches more than polished Clash with zero
  • Feedback from real users beats perfectionism
  • Launch a rough v1 on a shorter timeline rather than a polished v2 on a longer one

#22.9 Founder as Editor-in-Chief

  • AI drafts, human curates
  • No AI content published without review at launch
  • Review queue for lore, events, commissions
  • Editorial voice consistency maintained by a single authorial perspective

#22.10 NFT Holders as Equity Partners

  • Full commercial IP rights
  • Priority access to all future ecosystem benefits
  • Reputation follows the NFT, not the wallet
  • Every project decision either includes NFT holders or justifies why it doesn't

#22.11 Ecosystem Over Single Product

  • ROKI is a franchise, not a game
  • Clash + Zen + Dojo + Stare + Dodge + Breath + Train + Verse + Launchpad + NFT Generator + AR-Lite + Lands — all one universe
  • Each product reinforces the others
  • If one layer dies, the others survive and rebuild momentum
