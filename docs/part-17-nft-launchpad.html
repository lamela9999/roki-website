PART 17

NFT Launchpad

#17.1 Origin and Strategy

The NFT Launchpad is the mint infrastructure for ROKI. Every feature needed for ROKI is being built. Post-launch, the same infrastructure opens to other creators as a Phase 2 SaaS product.

#17.2 Differentiating Features

  • AI-powered NFT image generation built into the mint flow
  • Pre-finalization trait rerolls — solves a real creator pain point
  • Hybrid visual + metadata traits
  • Metaplex-compatible output for Solana

#17.3 Revenue Model

  • Platform takes a small per-mint commission
  • 100% of commission is swapped to $ROKI and burned
  • Exact fee percentage TBD

#17.4 Phase 1 vs Phase 2

Phase 1: Private to ROKI — build infrastructure, launch ROKI NFTs, prove the system with a real 3,333-piece collection, case study for Phase 2.

Phase 2: Public SaaS — open to external creators, begin marketing platform as independent product, burn mechanism activates on every external mint.

#17.5 Build Philosophy

Abstract architecture from day one. Every problem solved for ROKI's launch becomes a feature saleable to other creators later.

#17.6 Phase 2 Validation

Before opening publicly, conduct ~10-15 creator interviews.

#17.7 Legal Infrastructure

Terms of service, content policy, liability framework, tax reporting structure, lawyer consultation before Phase 2 launch.
