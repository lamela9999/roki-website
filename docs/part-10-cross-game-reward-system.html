PART 10

Cross-Game Reward System

#10.1 The Four Pillars of Progression

PillarSourceRewardCap/Reset
Reflex / SkillZen weekly leaderboard+1/+2 damage OR dmg takenWeekly reset
Reflex MasteryZen 3-Perfect run+1 permanent damage to chosen zone (Still Mind)One-time per NFT
Dedication / ConditioningDojo milestones+1 HP per milestone to chosen zoneCapped at +5 total
Strategy / ExperienceClash ELO + Fighter LegacyReputation, titles, canonical lore (no stat bonus)No cap

#10.2 Max Theoretical Combat Bonus

A maxed-out NFT fighter who is top 1 in Zen, has achieved Still Mind, and is fully Unyielding would have approximately +2 damage (or -2 taken) weekly (temporary), +1 permanent damage to one zone (Still Mind, permanent), and +5 HP distributed across zones (Dojo, permanent). Combined bonus over a base NFT: approximately 5-8% combat advantage. Noticeable, meaningful, but not decisive.

#10.3 Why Skill Comes Before Dedication

Skill rewards are larger and can repeat (weekly reset on Zen). Dedication rewards are smaller and capped (+5 HP total). Dedicated grinders cannot overtake skilled players, but dedicated players are not invisible.

#10.4 Cross-Game Stacking Rules

  • Bonuses from different pillars stack additively, not multiplicatively
  • No single pillar can exceed its individual cap
  • Minigames like Stare, Dodge, Breath, and Train contribute badges and streak titles, not combat stat bonuses
  • Future products should follow the same philosophy and be capped proportionally
