PART 20

Future Roadmap

#20.1 ROKI Lands (Grand Strategy — 18+ Months Out)

The long-term endgame. A grand strategy game where fighting (Clash) becomes the tactical layer and territorial control (Lands) becomes the strategic layer. Clans and outsiders compete for land, resources, and dominance over months-long campaigns. Strongest possible $ROKI sink.

#20.2 Season 2 Content: Environment Cards

15-20 card types that change battlefield rules for 2-3 rounds. Examples: The Pit (leg attacks unblockable), Fog (hidden attack zones), Quicksand (defense slots reduce each round).

#20.3 Season 3 Content: Technique Blockers

Passive immunities on some ROKIs. Certain technique types have no effect against these fighters. Creates deeper meta-game and counter-picking strategy.

#20.4 Potential Second NFT Drop: Companion Collection

Under consideration. A ROKI Beasts or Familiars companion collection. Pokémon trainer + Pokémon model. Gate mint to existing ROKI holders first.

#20.5 Animated Content

  • 30-second animated short film as flagship marketing
  • Telegram/Discord sticker packs
  • Twitter/X emote packs
  • Possible long-form animated series if budget permits

#20.6 Multi-Agent AI Build System

Longer-term experimental direction: using Claude Code as primary builder, other models as visual reviewers, with Puppeteer screenshots and shared knowledge base for automated feedback loops.
