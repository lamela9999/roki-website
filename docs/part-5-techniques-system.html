PART 5

Techniques System

#5.1 Overview

110 techniques exist in ROKI Clash, organized into three rarity tiers. Each ROKI has a loadout of 5 technique slots (Copy Ninjas get a 6th slot — see Part 6). The loadout is assigned at Fighter Creation and is permanent except through the Reforging mechanic (see Part 15).

#5.2 Rarity Tiers

  • Legendary: 13 techniques. Extremely powerful, often game-defining. NFT tier only.
  • Rare: 43 techniques. Strong, strategic. NFT and Paid tiers.
  • Common: 54 techniques. Solid baseline. All tiers.

#5.3 Loadout Formula

  • Every base NFT ROKI gets 2-3 Commons, 1-2 Rares, and ~30% chance of 1 Legendary
  • Most ROKIs have zero Legendaries; two Legendaries is a collector's item; three is almost mythical

#5.4 Cooldown Rules

Default cooldown is 2 rounds after use. Some techniques have longer cooldowns (3 or 4 rounds). Once-per-match techniques exist for the most extreme abilities.

#5.5 The 13 Legendary Techniques

  • Swap — Switch ALL zone HP values with opponent. Total HP reversal.
  • Resurrection — Zone KO'd to 0 HP comes back at 25 HP. Works once.
  • Power Absorb — Every block permanently reduces opponent's damage for that zone by 5. Caps at -15 per zone.
  • Power Surge — Every clean hit permanently increases your damage for that zone by 5. Caps at +15 per zone.
  • Iron Will — No zone can drop below 10 HP. Can only lose by points decision.
  • Berserker Mode — Permanently block 1 zone but attack damage increased 50%.
  • Clone — Attack 2 zones AND defend 3 zones at full power this round. 4-round cooldown.
  • Weakening Aura — All opponent attacks do 10 less damage permanently.
  • Fortress Mode — Permanently block 3 zones instead of 2. Attack damage reduced 25%.
  • Executioner — If attack hits zone below 50 HP that is undefended, double damage.
  • Condemned — Mark opponent zone. If not healed above 70 HP in 3 rounds, drops to 10 HP.
  • Momentum Lock — If you win a round cleanly, next round attack is unblockable.
  • Bloodlust — Every undefended hit gives +5 permanent stacking attack bonus. Caps at +15 per zone.

#5.6 The 43 Rare Techniques

  • Double Strike — Attack 2 zones at full damage this round.
  • Ambush — No attack this round. Next round 2 zones at full damage, both unblockable.
  • Precision Strike — Bypasses one of opponent's defense slots.
  • Combo — If attack lands undefended, next round auto-targets same zone unblockable.
  • Bulldoze — One of opponent's two blocks randomly disappears this round.
  • Crippling Blow — If attack lands undefended, healing blocked on that zone for 4 rounds.
  • Haymaker — Double damage this round. (3-round cooldown.)
  • Fortress — Block 3 zones instead of 2 AND still attack normally.
  • Parry — If you block opponent's attack, steal 10 HP and add to your weakest zone.
  • Stone Body — All damage received split equally across all 4 zones.
  • Lock — Pick one opponent technique, disabled rest of match permanently.
  • Shackle — Opponent's strongest technique automatically disabled for 3 rounds.
  • Silence — Opponent cannot use any techniques next round.
  • Suppress — Opponent's style mechanic disabled this round.
  • Skull Crack — +15 to head attacks this round.
  • Gut Punch — +15 to body attacks this round.
  • Knee Destroyer — +15 to leg attacks this round.
  • Shoulder Break — +15 to arm attacks this round.
  • Finisher — If zone below 30 HP, attack goes through even if blocked.
  • Vital Strike — +15 if you attack opponent's lowest HP zone.
  • Blood Exchange — Take 15 to highest zone, heal 30 to lowest zone.
  • Survival Instinct — If any zone below 20 HP, it heals 25.
  • Lifeline — When any zone drops below 25 HP, all damage to that zone reduced 50% rest of match.
  • Purify — Remove all negative effects AND heal lowest zone by 10.
  • Reflect — Whatever damage you receive, exact same applied to same zone on opponent.
  • Steal — If opponent's attack blocked, their technique on 4-round cooldown and you copy it once.
  • Anticipate — Predict opponent's attack zone. Correct = their attack does 0, yours +20.
  • Confidence — If you won last round, attack does +20 this round.
  • Equalizer — If opponent total HP higher, +25% of HP difference as bonus damage.
  • Desperation — If your total HP lower than opponent, +10 damage for 3 rounds.
  • Poison — Opponent loses 5 HP per round from chosen zone, rest of match.
  • Curse — Opponent healing techniques disabled rest of match.
  • Seal — Opponent disruption techniques disabled rest of match.
  • Hunter's Mark — Mark one opponent zone. +10 permanent bonus to your attacks on that zone.
  • Predator — Permanently hide your exact HP numbers from your opponent — they still see your HP bars, but not the numbers. Lasts the rest of the match.
  • Paralyze — If attack lands undefended, opponent can't defend next round.
  • Time Bomb — Place bomb on opponent zone. Explodes for 40 damage in 3 rounds. Removable by Purify.
  • Phantom — Opponent's attack guaranteed miss. You attack normally. (4-round cooldown.)
  • Final Stand — Below 150 total HP: +15 damage and block 3 zones for 2 rounds.
  • Stall — No damage for 2 rounds. All cooldowns refresh by 2 rounds.
  • Fortify — One zone permanently gains +25 max HP.
  • Collapse — One opponent zone permanently loses 15 max HP.
  • Siphon — For 3 rounds, heal lowest zone by 30% of damage dealt.

#5.7 The 54 Common Techniques

  • Cheap Shot — Normal damage plus disables one opponent technique for 2 rounds.
  • Lunge — +20 damage any zone but defense reduced to 1 block this round.
  • Sucker Punch — If opponent used healing this round, your attack does double damage.
  • Heavy Slam — +10 damage but your arms take 5 self-damage.
  • Iron Skin — All damage received reduced by 50% this round.
  • Absorb — If hit, heal a different zone by same amount.
  • Deflect — If you block opponent's attack, their next round attack does 50% less.
  • Dodge — If opponent hits undefended zone, damage reduced by 50%.
  • Brace — All damage received delayed by 2 rounds.
  • Last Stand — If any zone in red (<40 HP), attack does +15 bonus.
  • Scramble — Opponent's defense choices randomly reassigned.
  • Trap — Pick a zone. If opponent attacks it, they take 25 damage to arms.
  • Fatigue — All opponent cooldowns extended by 1 round.
  • Shatter — If attack hits undefended zone, that zone permanently takes +5 from future attacks.
  • Disable — Pick one opponent zone. They cannot attack that zone this round.
  • Delay — Opponent's attack damage applied 2 rounds later.
  • Expose — See opponent's attack zone before you lock in defense.
  • Head Hunter — +10 to head attacks this round.
  • Body Breaker — +10 to body attacks this round.
  • Leg Sweep — +10 to leg attacks this round.
  • Arm Lock — +10 to arm attacks this round.
  • Whiplash — Normal damage plus that zone can't be in opponent's blocks next round.
  • Second Wind — Heal lowest zone by 25.
  • Adrenaline — Heal all four zones by 10 each.
  • Regenerate — Pick one zone. Heals 8 this round and 8 next round.
  • Drain — If attack lands, heal zone you attacked from by 15.
  • Patch Up — Pick one zone. Cannot drop below 1 HP for 2 rounds.
  • Endure — For 3 rounds all incoming damage reduced by 10.
  • One-Two — Full damage to one zone. If undefended, second hit half damage random zone.
  • Setup — Half damage this round. Attacked zone marked, next round +20 to that zone.
  • Chain — If you hit undefended this and last round, +15 bonus.
  • Follow Through — If blocked, redirects to random undefended zone at half damage.
  • Thorns — Any damage received, attacker takes 50% back to same zone.
  • Punish — If opponent misses (blocked), they take 20 damage to arms.
  • War Cry — Next 2 attacks do +5 each.
  • Rage — Every time you take damage this round, next attack +5 stacking.
  • Relentless Pressure — Consecutive undefended hits give permanent +3. Streak resets on miss.
  • Vengeance — If you took damage last round, +50% of what you received as bonus.
  • Untouchable — Blocked last round and this round = next attack unblockable.
  • Switch — Swap HP values of two of your own zones.
  • Transfer — Move 25 HP from one of your zones to another.
  • Guard — Pick one zone. Half damage for 5 rounds.
  • Reposition — Swap HP values of two opponent zones.
  • Armor — First hit each round does 10 less damage. Rest of match.
  • Resilience — Arm and leg cripple effects halved.
  • Adaptation — If opponent attacks same zone twice in row, second does 50% less.
  • Blackout — For 2 rounds both players lose all technique access.
  • Mirror Match — For 3 rounds both players have identical damage regardless of style.
  • Calm — All your cooldowns reduced by 2 rounds.
  • Mind Game — See which technique opponent selected this round.
  • Lockdown — Both players must repeat same attack and defense as last round.
  • Debt — +30 damage this round. Cannot attack next round.
  • Ricochet — If attack blocked, damage bounces to random undefended zone.
  • All or Nothing — Double damage or zero damage. 50/50 chance.
