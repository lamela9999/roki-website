PART 19

Launch Strategy

#19.1 Strategic Overview

Token launches first. Game follows. NFT mint happens later, to an already-engaged player base, once market conditions and community are right.

#19.2 Phase 1: Quiet Build (Current)

  • Finish ROKI Clash to playable beta
  • Build the AI art generator as standalone utility
  • Build the NFT Launchpad infrastructure (includes the NFT Generator — see 19.11)
  • Prepare update pipeline: 2-3 months of announceable content banked in advance
  • Internal community of ~70 trusted early supporters

#19.3 Phase 2: Token Launch

Pre-announce treasury accumulation. Disclose policy framework. Token launches on pump.fun. Initial hype cycle with the ~70-person early community. Treasury trims on highs at key price points, funds development.

#19.4 Phase 3: Quiet Period

Let initial hype cycle settle. Mercenary flippers exit. Real believers accumulate at lower prices. Project signals continued development without burning banked announcement content.

#19.5 Phase 4: Announcement Ramp

Start releasing banked updates. Early teasers: sneak peeks of the game, character designs, gameplay clips. Momentum-building through consistent delivery. Treasury buybacks during drawdowns.

#19.6 Phase 5: Game Beta (Free-to-Play, Pre-NFT)

ROKI Clash opens as free-to-play with placeholder characters. Community builds around the game, not speculation. Discord/Telegram forms organically. Top 500-1000 players become the pre-qualified NFT mint whitelist.

#19.7 Phase 6: NFT Mint

Launch NFT mint once token market cap and player community justify it. Priority whitelist: active Phase 5 players. Mint uses the ROKI Launchpad infrastructure (proof-of-concept for Phase 7).

#19.8 Phase 7: Ecosystem Expansion

ROKI Zen launches. ROKI Dojo launches. ROKI Verse lore system activates. NFT Launchpad opens to external creators (Phase 2 of the Launchpad product). AI Image Generator launches. First tournaments run.

#19.9 Phase 8: Mainstream Expansion

Free tier fully opens. Paid tier launches with $20-50 character purchase. Rookie League onboarding. Token-staking Challenger Cup qualification activates.

#19.10 Phase 9 and Beyond

ROKI Lands (18+ months out). Collaborations and partnerships. Seasonal Chronicles published quarterly. Potential second NFT drop (Beasts/Familiars) under consideration.

#19.11 Slotting the v3 Tonight-Built Products

NFT Generator (internal tool):

  • Phase 1 — must be complete before NFT mint (Phase 6). The Generator IS the mint production pipeline.
  • Phase 7 — repackaged as external launchpad SaaS alongside the platform opening to other creators.

ROKI Zen, Dojo, Stare, Dodge, Breath, Train:

  • Ship in waves leading up to or just after the NFT mint as engagement surfaces.
  • Zen and Dojo are Phase 7 anchors (already planned).
  • Stare and Dodge are high-virality, low-complexity builds — ship between Phase 5 (Game Beta) and Phase 6 (Mint) to attract eyeballs.
  • Breath and Train are retention surfaces — ship around or after Phase 6 and 7 to extend daily engagement for the early holder base.

AR-Lite Photo Maker:

  • Ship at Phase 6 (Mint) as day-one organic marketing tool. The primary use case is holders taking AR-lite photos with their fresh NFT on mint day. Without this in place at mint, the project loses a one-time free-virality moment.

#19.12 Critical Execution Principles

  • NEVER announce features that aren't built
  • Maintain 2-3 months of banked updates ahead of announcements
  • Transparent treasury operations with published policy
  • Ship over polish: rough Clash with 50 real players beats polished Clash with zero players
  • Build for long-term franchise, not short-term pump
