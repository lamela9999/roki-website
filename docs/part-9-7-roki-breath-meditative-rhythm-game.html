PART 9.7

ROKI Breath (Meditative Rhythm Game)

NEW IN v3 — the meditative companion to high-adrenaline Zen and Clash.

#9.7.1 Concept

A visual circle expands and contracts. The player presses-and-holds during the inhale phase (circle expanding) and releases during the exhale phase (circle contracting). Maintaining the rhythm builds a streak. A disrupted rhythm breaks the combo.

#9.7.2 Scoring

  • Total complete breath cycles per session (a full inhale/exhale loop held correctly = 1 cycle)
  • Longest unbroken streak within a session

#9.7.3 Daily Streak Counter

  • If the player does at least one Breath session per day, the daily streak increments by 1.
  • A skipped day resets the streak to 0. No grace period; no paid revive.
  • Daily streak is the primary retention loop.

#9.7.4 Achievement (NFT-only, permanent)

  • The Mountain — 30-day daily streak. Written to Fighter Legacy.

#9.7.5 Why It’s in the Ecosystem

  • Pre-fight breathing mastery — fighters at every level use breath control as a performance tool.
  • A meditative counterweight to the adrenaline-forward tone of Zen and Clash.
  • Extremely low barrier to entry; high retention potential via daily streak psychology.
  • Wellness surface that the brand can discuss without feeling off-theme.

#9.7.6 Tier Access

  • All tiers can play.
  • Permanent achievements (The Mountain) are NFT-only.
