How Roki Radar works

1 · Ingest

Parsed transactions

Pull parsed Solana history (Helius), build a directed transfer graph, and strip hubs (DEXs, routers, CEXs) before any clustering. Skipping that collapses the graph into one blob.

2 · Resolve

Entity resolution

Six signals - shared rent, Jito bundles, direct transfers, shared funders, time-sync, fingerprint - combine with a noisy-OR. Independence splits one operator's wallet farm from genuine collusion.

3 · Score

Plus-minus + risk

Log-return trade scores with empirical-Bayes shrinkage rank entities fairly. Bag-worked PnL (dumping on your own callers) is tagged dirty and excluded from alpha boards.

4 · Narrate

Plain-English verdict

The AI narrator turns the computed signals into a verdict. The lens you pick reweights the score and shifts emphasis - same data, different framing. It never claims certainty.

v1 is read-only and non-custodial. No trade execution, no embedded wallet, no fiat. "Connect wallet" means sign-to-claim-identity only. Live scans pull real market data (DexScreener) and mint/freeze authority (Solana RPC) on-chain. Entity clustering, funding trace, and PnL leaderboards run on the Helius-powered backend and are rolling out — sample tokens show worked examples of those.
