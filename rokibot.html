The intelligence

A shared brain runs once, centrally; every bot reads from it. The edge is whose action you react to — and not getting faked out by manufactured "smart money."

Wallet labeling — the moat

classify wallets, follow funding lineage

Smart money signal

Consistent net-positive realized PnL. A buy here is real signal.

Insiders / devs mixed

Linked to creation/early funding. Accumulation = signal; first sell = hardest exit trigger.

Fresh / bot noise

New, no track record. Down-weight or ignore.

Cluster trap

Many wallets funded from one source, moving in sync. Manufactured "smart money" — a manipulation tell, treated as negative.

A+ setup: a genuine net-positive wallet accumulating into a price dip.
The trap: a cluster of fresh wallets pumping in sync — looks identical, isn't. Caught by following funding lineage (who funded whom), not a static address list.
The edge is a data-freshness race (seconds-to-minutes on already-public data), not an MEV/latency race. We don't front-run the mempool.

The brain — centralized learning

learns on paper + history, never on your deposits

measure signals, not trades

"When smart-money-buys-the-dip fired across 200 tokens, what was the hit rate?" — not "did this one trade win?"

sizing ≠ selection

Bet size stays flat while gathering evidence. Never size up on a win streak — that's the classic blow-up.

graduate & kill

A signal stays at small test size until it clears a sample-count + expectancy bar. Drifts net-negative → cut it.

regime-aware

Rolling stats read the current market regime; weights shift with it. Used mostly on defense — dialing every bot's aggression down when conditions turn hostile.

Non-custodial & safe

The bot trades on a separate wallet you fund — never your main wallet — and the platform itself cannot move your money.

How custody works

enclave signing + policy engine

  • A fresh per-bot trading wallet; you fund it from your main wallet
  • The key lives in a secure hardware enclave; the bot gets signatures, never the raw key — so it can run 24/7
  • Policy: may sign swaps through the DEX router (Jupiter) only; may not transfer funds anywhere except your registered withdrawal address
  • You can withdraw or revoke anytime

Circuit breakers

limits you set, enforced server-side

  • Kill switch — stop all trading instantly
  • Daily loss limit — auto-halt past a threshold
  • Per-position + slippage caps from your risk dial
  • DENY rules override ALLOW as hard circuit-breakers

Faith-conscious & honest

Built on the ROKI ecosystem's halal-conscious principles: no gambling mechanics, no pay-to-win. The product is framed as a risk-posture execution tool, not a guaranteed money-maker.
